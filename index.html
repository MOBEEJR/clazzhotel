About Clazz Hotel & Lounge

Welcome to CLAZZ Hotel and Lounge, a luxurious sanctuary nestled in the heart of Victoria Island, Lagos. Located at 22 Trinity Avenue, Oniru, CLAZZ blends modern sophistication with timeless elegance, offering an unforgettable hospitality experience. At CLAZZ, we believe in excellence. Whether you're here for business, leisure, or a special celebration, our world-class rooms and suites, exceptional dining experiences, and serene lounge spaces are designed to provide comfort, class, and convenience. Our dedicated staff delivers impeccable service with a personal touch, ensuring every guest feels valued and at home. From our beautifully furnished rooms to our high-end recreational facilities, every detail is carefully curated to reflect the CLAZZ standard. Discover a new level of luxury. Discover CLAZZ.
